What SplitTable is

SplitTable lets verified adults discover, host and join group bookings at restaurants, bars and clubs ("tables"). The host books the venue; joiners apply, are reviewed by the host, and on approval split the bill at agreed terms. Currently in staging, all tables are free; paid tables will be reintroduced once our French commercial registration (SIRET) is finalised.

Your responsibilities

  • Honesty — accurate profile, real photos, no impersonation.
  • Show up — if you're approved on a table, you commit to attending. Cancellations within 6 hours of the start time damage trust scores.
  • Safety — meet in the public venue, never share home addresses, follow local laws and the venue's house rules.
  • No commercial use — SplitTable is for personal social bookings, not for promoting businesses or services.

  • We're not a party to the booking itself — we facilitate the introduction; the table experience happens between you, your co-attendees, and the venue.
